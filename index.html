Summary

I hold a B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering, specializing in Hardware Engineering, from the University of Sistan and Baluchestan, Iran. I have worked as a teaching assistant in the ECE department for almost two years (2018-2020) and guided students in six different undergraduate subjects. Currently, I am working as a research assistant at University of Sistan and Baluchestan, participating in a research project related to multi-objective design space exploration for FPGA-based system design.

I am primarily interested in digital system design for different applications such as DSP, computer networks, artificial intelligence, and healthcare technology. Most of my prior experiences are in the field of FPGA-based system design using Verilog and VHDL, and I enjoy studying reconfigurable architectures. In addition, I have worked with software programming languages such as C++ and C#, and I am proficient in structural and object-oriented programming.
